Linn County Clarion (Mound City, Kansas) — June 7, 1889

Big Family Dinner — 36 Relatives Present

Linn County Republic (Mound City, Kansas) — July 12, 1918

There were 36 relatives present. It was a pleasant visit for all as it had been a long time since they had met together.
