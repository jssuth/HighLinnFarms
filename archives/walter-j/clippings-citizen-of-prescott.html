Sells His Interest in the Mill

Prescott Eagle (Prescott, Kansas) — May 10, 1884

J. T. Maupin, M. C. Stark and J. H. Bell are now the proprietors of the Prescott Mills, having purchased Walter Sutherland's interest in the property. The roller process will be added to the attractions of the mills, which will be ready for business in about three weeks.

The Most Beautiful Team in Town

Prescott Eagle (Prescott, Kansas) — August 23, 1884
