Our Mission

At High Linn Farms, we believe in preserving the legacy of rural living while embracing sustainable practices for tomorrow. Our mission is to cultivate community, honor tradition, and grow with purpose.

Our Story Begins Here

High Linn Farms is part of a family heritage that reaches back more than 150 years in the Prescott, Kansas area. Our great-grandfather, a Union soldier in the Civil War, settled on the Kansas side of the state line 4 miles east in 1869. The current farm has been in our family for over 75 years, first established by my father.

Located on the southeastern edge of the Kansas City/Overland Park metro region, High Linn Farms still reflects the character of an earlier time. Most elements of the original 1910-era development remain, built when horsepower, not engines, powered daily life. Today, our goal is to preserve and restore these historic structures while caring for the land for future generations.
